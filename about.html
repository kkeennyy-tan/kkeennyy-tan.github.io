About Company


Good Heart Food and Industrial Corporation


The Company was established in 2010. The primary purpose of its establishment was to ensure supply to its' valued clients. The company has been one of the leading companies in the ice cream cone business and serves the major players of ice cream industries in the Philippines. In doing so, the management acquired latest European machines and technology to manufacture high quality and international standard products and a set of delivery vans to guarantee prompt delivery to various customers around Metro Manila and nearby provinces. Presently, the company produces not only ice cream cones but also an assortment of scrumptious baked goods for prominent institutional accounts and convenience stores. It is also the first ice cream cone manufacturer in the Philippines to be certified in ISO 22000:2005 Food Safety Management System. The company was certified just recently due to its conformity to the food safety management system.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission to be the premier manufacturer and preferred supplier of quality cone products to consumer product manufacturers in the Philippines as well as in global market.


VISION: Our vision to supply our customers with reliable service and superior quality of cones at competitive prices through strict adherence to high quality standard of manufacturing.
